## Service Organization 
Service organization exists inside a company and is the owner of the Requests, Request Contract and Request Quotations and offers services defined in the Service Catalog.

A Service Organization must be created with default Time zone site, Logistic Site and a Sales Site.

## SLA (Service lever Agreement)

A commitment between service provider and the customer that defines how quickly and how well a service request must be handled.
in IFS SLAs are used to track monitor and enforce these service performance commitments.
- Calendar 
- Work Stages
- SLA Scheduling Types 
- Event

- Duration (defined Time)
- Calendar (Calendar)
